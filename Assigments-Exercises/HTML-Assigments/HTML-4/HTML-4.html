Top 3 Things I Need for My Hobby

  • Chess Board
  • The Chess Pieces
  • Chess Book
  • Chess Clock

Steps to Get Started

  1. Step 1: Place the chess board wherever you like and arrange the chess pieces into the chess board.
  2. Step 2: Assign a time for the chess clock and place it beside the chess board.
  3. Step 3: Open the chess book and start learning!
